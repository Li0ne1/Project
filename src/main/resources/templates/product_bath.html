Taking its inspiration from the silhouette of our iconic and versatile Lopez loafer, Bath brings some freshness to our collection. The intricate hand stitching details on the saddle and the apron echo to glove making, marking a new chapter for the house’s casual offer. This stitching technique is an aesthetic feature but also a technical detail providing real comfort and softness. The unlined construction provides unmatched lightness for a goodyear welted shoe. It fits like a glove!

How to wear

A supple, summertime loafer, Bath tends towards a certain sartorial nonchalance and relaxed style. It’s perfect with jeans, or with chinos. Also, with Bermudas or swimming shorts. And remember that, as a general rule, Bath looks better without socks than with them.
